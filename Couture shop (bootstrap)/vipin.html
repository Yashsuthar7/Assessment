TAJ MAHAL

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the south bank of the Yamuna river in the Indian city of Agra. The Taj Mahal attracts more than 6 million visitors a year and in 2007, it was declared a winner of the New 7 Wonders of the World (2000–2007) initiative.The so-called “22 rooms” in the basement of the Taj Mahal are not really rooms

STATUE OF UNITY

The statue has been built as an ode to the Iron Man of India, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, the first home minister of independent India. He was responsible for uniting all 562 princely states of the country to build the Republic of India. Inside the Sardar Patel Statue complex, the government has built 3-star hotels, shopping centers, and a research center.

HAWA MAHAL

Hawa Mahal is famous because of its amazing and unique architecture. 953 windows or jharokhas are beautifully incorporated in the honeycomb design of this palace. In addition to providing a window to the outside world for the royal women, these windows helped in keeping the palace airy and cool.

Last updated 3 mins ago

VIETNAM

Vietnam is known for being a popular tourist destination because of its beautiful beaches, it's culture, amazing food (like pho), and friendly people. Vietnam is also famous for the Vietnam War, motorbikes (86% of households have one), Vietnamese coffee, floating markets and rice terraces.

Last updated 3 mins ago

BALI INDONESIA

the Land of the Gods, Bali appeals through its sheer natural beauty of looming volcanoes and lush terraced rice fields that exude peace and serenity. It is also famous for surfers' paradise! Bali is a highly spiritual place. It is an island where East meets West and where Christianity, Islam, Buddhism and Hinduism co-exist peacefully next to each other.

Last updated 3 mins ago

WEST BENGAL

The South Indian culture is celebrated in the elaborate dance forms of South India: Koodiyattam, Bharatanatyam, Oyilattam, Karakattam, Kuchipudi, Kathakali, Thirayattam, Theyyam, Bhuta Kola, Ottamthullal, Oppana, Kerala Natanam, Mohiniaattam and Yakshagana. There is not one but many famous dishes in South India including Pongal,Hyderabadi biryani and many more.
